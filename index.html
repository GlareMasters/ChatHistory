June (7) - VIP 22, Cores (Nike, Apollo), March 14, Sh23 buildings, Gem preset, Paladin of Sola Gear

August - Graveyard upgrade

September - Cores (Bacchus), gear workshop, Sh24 buildings

October - hero 70, Cores (Lady Justice), garrison, sh24 buildings


Business Effect:
Players who wanted to play longer but are discouraged due to the upgrades are lost business. They are mostly players spending in the game regardless of amount. Enough upgrade pacing means longer revenue from that upgrade and longer life span for the game.

>Cost
The cost of each upgrade is too much that even big spenders are feeling the burnt in their pockets. This too discourages players. If only upgrades would require just a couple of packs instead of 10s or 100s pack, then players will be more happy. The more players can upgrade, the more active

b. SYSTEM/SERVER ISSUES
>Lags
Lag has improved a bit from last year. But there is still lags, which annoy players, especially during events like SW, coliseum or kvk where lag is worst. Aside from hindering players to play smoothly, it also costs money. Many players won't join events due to lag. Why not eliminate other unnecessary steps to prevent lags like warrant pop-up, multiple buttons in joining rallies, etc.

>Glitches
Too many glitches in the game that haven't been fixed for months such as tile porting, heroless, skill preset, core not registering, on and off VIP, etc. These glitches are irritating and at the same times costs so much money. It costs so much to upgrade, fir example, to VIP 24 only to have it glitched (on and off) and waste cores.

>App Crash
Game crashes especially during events must be minimal, if not totally eliminated.

Business effect:
If lags and glitches are addressed, more players will join events. The more active the game is, the more revenue for MZ as players will purchase items they need for the event.

c. CUSTOMER SERVICE
>More responsive
Players send letter with the hope that MZ will respond and address concerns. Sadly, many mails are ignored for days. Mails must be answered within 24hrs not by autoreply but real custoner service agent.

>Quality of response
Complaints on glitches can be validated in your logs. Why not compensate players in full who suffered from the glitches? Compensating us with a fraction of what was lost actually just give a feeling of insults or irritates players.

d. EVENTS
>Interval and duration
Kvk is one of the most anticipated and enjoyed parts of the game. Yet it only happens every 2-3wks. This makes players bored in the game. Making it more frequent like weekly and in longer duration like 24hrs to cover players from different timezones

>Coliseum events
Ports for the coliseum event must be earned from infernos and/or bought with gold, loyalty points or copper not just in packs. Coliseums for different power levels must be returned so that even small players can enjoy the event.

Business Effect:
Players, whether small, medium or big spender, spend in preparation for kvk. Giving more events will encourage them to spend and grow.

e. PACKS
>Content
Current packs are a lot better than last year. However, contents in most packs are outdated. Items that are in demand and highly needed are often than not absent in packs. Why not remove chests and items that are outdated and replace them with new ones like time orbs, blessing orbs, heroic medals, gems, etc.

>Price
Put ancient advanced teleport in $5-$20 or increase numbers in bigger pack.

f. MONSTERS AND TILES
>Drops
Make drops from monsters relevant and update too. Gems, pcs, cores or materials that are new and in demand.

OTHER SUGGESTIONS:
>Alliance City-Bazaar
The alliance city is a nice adornment in the game but it should be made useful. The bazaar in particular should enable players to trade items between themselves. Copper should be varying or increasing. Items that can be sold and bought must be replaced/updated 2x or once a month.
